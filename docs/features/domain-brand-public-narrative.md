# Domain: Brand + Public Narrative

## Why

This domain exists so what the world sees—name, story, visual system, and public claims—stays coherent across LuXeel, UNBND, and 3DPD, and does not drift into code or growth assets without authority.

## Scope

### In scope

- Brand architecture, narrative, public-site messaging, and approval for externally visible claims.
- Guidelines that Client Experience, CRM + Growth, and Creator surfaces must follow.

### Out of scope

- Private operational messaging (Operations Console).
- Implementation of UI components (owned by respective product domains; this domain sets constraints).

## Owner

### Accountable

Brand / executive sponsor for narrative; legal review for any regulated or comparative claims.

### Consulted

Compliance + Legal, Client Experience, CRM + Growth, Creator Console.

## Dependencies

### Soft dependencies

- Compliance + Legal: approvals on sensitive statements.
- Client Experience / CRM + Growth: application of guidelines in shipping surfaces.

### Hard dependencies

- None technical; authority dependencies are governance-first.

### Temporal dependencies

- Rebrands or major announcements: time-boxed approval windows and freeze coordination with Growth launches.
