# Domain: CRM + Growth

## Why

This domain exists to own lifecycle, attribution, experiments, and communications that drive acquisition and retention—without polluting core transactional domains or misrepresenting brand and compliance constraints.

## Scope

### In scope

- CRM objects, segments, campaigns, and in-product growth surfaces where they are explicitly growth-owned.
- Experimentation metadata and success metrics owned by growth.

### Out of scope

- Checkout and ledger correctness (Payments + Tax).
- Legal disclosures and regulated claims (Compliance + Legal; Brand for public voice).
- Core product IA for non-growth flows (Client Experience / Creator Console own primary journeys).

## Owner

### Accountable

Growth / marketing leadership; messaging that implies legal or financial claims requires Compliance + Legal and Brand review.

### Consulted

Brand + Public Narrative, Compliance + Legal (data use), Client Experience / Creator Console (surface area), Infrastructure + AI Governance (email/SMS/integrations).

## Dependencies

### Hard dependencies

- Identity + Verification: stable ids and consent flags for outreach where required.

### Soft dependencies

- Payments + Tax: segments based on purchase behavior.
- Operations Console: handoff when campaigns generate support load.

### Temporal dependencies

- Launches and bursts: short-lived experiments with explicit sunset dates.
