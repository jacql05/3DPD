# Domain: Client Experience

## Why

This domain exists so buyers and clients have a consistent, trustworthy path from discovery through purchase and delivery—aligned with brand promise and separated from creator tools and internal ops.

## Scope

### In scope

- Client-facing discovery, purchase, onboarding, and delivery status experiences.
- Client support entry points that are productized (not raw ops tools).

### Out of scope

- Creator production workflows (Creator Console).
- Internal queues and admin actions (Operations Console).
- Growth automation and CRM bulk logic (CRM + Growth).

## Owner

### Accountable

Product owner for client experience; revenue-impacting flows align with Payments + Tax.

### Consulted

Brand + Public Narrative (voice and claims), Compliance + Legal (consumer terms), Payments + Tax (checkout), Identity + Verification (client accounts).

## Dependencies

### Hard dependencies

- Payments + Tax for checkout and contracts tied to money.
- Identity + Verification for client accounts where required.

### Soft dependencies

- CRM + Growth for personalized journeys.
- Infrastructure + AI Governance for performance, security, and optional AI-assisted shopping aids.

### Temporal dependencies

- Seasonal campaigns: may require coordinated Brand + CRM windows.
